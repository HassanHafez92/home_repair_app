## Getting Started

### Creating Your Technician Account

1. **Download the App**: Install from App Store or Google Play
2. **Open the App** and tap **"Technician Signup"**
3. **Fill in Registration Form**:
   - Full Name
   - Email and Password
   - Phone Number
   - Expertise/Services (select all that apply)
   - Years of Experience
   - ID/License Number (for verification)
4. **Upload Documents**:
   - Profile Photo
   - ID Card/Driver's License
   - Professional Certification (if applicable)
5. Tap **"Submit Application"**

### Account Approval

- Your application will be reviewed within **24-48 hours**
- You'll receive an email notification when approved
- Check application status in the app

### First-Time Login

1. Open the app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Tap **"Sign In as Technician"**
4. Complete your profile setup

## Accepting Jobs

### New Order Notification

When a new order matches your services:
1. You'll receive a **push notification**
2. Notification shows:
   - Service type
   - Location distance from you
   - Scheduled time
   - Visit fee

### Reviewing the Order

1. Tap the notification to open order details
2. Review:
   - **Service**: Can you perform this repair?
   - **Photos**: Do you have the right tools/parts?
   - **Location**: Is it within your service area?
   - **Schedule**: Are you available?

### Accept or Decline

**To Accept**:
1. Tap **"Accept Order"**
2. Confirm your availability
3. Order moves to your **Active Jobs**
4. Customer is notified immediately

**To Decline**:
1. Tap **"Decline"**
2. Order is offered to another technician

> **Tip**: High acceptance rates improve your profile ranking

### After Acceptance

- Chat becomes available
- Customer can see your profile
- Order appears in your schedule
- You can view the customer's location on the map

## Updating Order Status

### Status Workflow

Update the order status as you progress:

1. **Confirmed** üü¢ (automatically set when you accept)
   - You've accepted the job

2. **Traveling** üöó
   - Tap **"Start Traveling"** when you leave for the job
   - Customer can see you're on the way

3. **Arrived** üìç
   - Tap **"I've Arrived"** when you reach the location
   - Notifies the customer

4. **Working** üîß
   - Tap **"Start Work"** when you begin the repair
   - Timer starts (optional, for your records)

5. **Completed** ‚úÖ
   - Tap **"Mark as Complete"** when finished
   - Enter final price and completion notes
   - Customer is prompted to pay and review

### Completing an Order

When the job is done:

1. Tap **"Complete Order"**
2. **Enter Final Price**:
   - Service cost
   - Parts used (itemized)
   - Total (Visit fee + Service + VAT)
3. **Add Completion Notes**:
   - What was fixed
   - Any recommendations for the customer
4. **Upload Completion Photos** (optional):
   - Show the finished work
5. Tap **"Submit"**

Customer will review and approve payment.

### Cancelling an Order

If you must cancel:

1. Go to the order details
2. Tap **"Cancel Order"**
3. Select a reason:
   - Emergency
   - Unable to fix
   - Parts not available
   - Other
4. Provide details
5. Confirm cancellation

> **Warning**: Frequent cancellations may affect your account standing

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**  
A: After order completion and customer payment approval. Withdraw funds anytime.

**Q: What if the customer doesn't pay?**  
A: Payment is handled through the app. Contact support if there's an issue.

**Q: Can I set my own prices?**  
A: Visit fees and base prices are platform-set. You can adjust final price based on work complexity.

**Q: How do I handle disputes?**  
A: Contact support immediately. Go to **"Help"** ‚Üí **"Report Issue"**

**Q: What if I need to reschedule?**  
A: Chat with the customer to agree on a new time, then update the order schedule.
