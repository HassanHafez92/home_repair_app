## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Download the App**: Install from App Store or Google Play
2. **Open the App**: Tap the app icon to launch
3. **Sign Up**:
   - Tap **"Create Account"**
   - Enter your email and password
   - Fill in your name and phone number
   - Tap **"Register"**
4. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox and verify your email address

### Logging In

1. Open the app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Tap **"Sign In"**

**Forgot Password?**
- Tap **"Forgot Password"**
- Enter your email
- Check your inbox for reset link

## Browsing Services

### View All Services

1. From the **Home Screen**, swipe through the service carousel
2. Or tap **"All Services"** to see the complete list

### Search for a Service

1. Tap the **search icon** at the top
2. Type the service name (e.g., "plumbing", "electrical")
3. Tap on a service to view details

### Service Details

When you tap on a service, you'll see:
- **Description**: What's included in the service
- **Price Range**: Estimated cost (SAR 150 - 500)
- **Visit Fee**: Technician visit/inspection fee (SAR 50)
- **Average Time**: How long the service usually takes

## Booking a Service

### Step-by-Step Booking

#### Step 1: Select Service
1. Tap on the service you need (e.g., "AC Repair")
2. Review the details
3. Tap **"Book Now"**

####  Step 2: Describe the Problem
1. Enter a detailed description of the issue
2. **Add Photos** (optional but recommended):
   - Tap **"Add Photo"**
   - Take a photo or select from gallery
   - Add up to 5 photos
3. Tap **"Next"**

#### Step 3: Choose Location
**Option A: Select Saved Address**
- Choose from your saved addresses
- Or tap **"Add New Address"**

**Option B: Pick on Map**
- Tap **"Select on Map"**
- Move the map to your location
- Tap **"Confirm Location"**
- Address will be auto-filled

#### Step 4: Schedule Date & Time
1. Select your preferred date
2. Choose a time slot (e.g., "Morning 9 AM - 12 PM")
3. Tap **"Next"**

#### Step 5: Review & Confirm
1. Review all details:
   - Service name
   - Problem description
   - Location
   - Scheduled time
   - Pricing breakdown
2. Select payment method (Cash, Card, or Wallet)
3. Tap **"Confirm Booking"**

## Leaving a Review

### After Order Completion

1. You'll receive a notification: "Please rate your service"
2. Tap the notification or go to **Past Orders**
3. Tap on the completed order
4. Tap **"Leave a Review"**

### Writing Your Review

1. **Rate Overall Quality** (1-5 stars):
   - 5 = Excellent
   - 4 = Good
   - 3 = Average
   - 2 = Below Average
   - 1 = Poor

2. **Rate Specific Categories** (optional):
   - Quality of Work
   - Professionalism
   - Punctuality
   - Value for Money

3. **Write a Comment** (optional):
   - Share your experience
   - Be specific about what was good or could be improved

4. **Add Photos** (optional):
   - Show the completed work
   - Up to 5 photos

5. Tap **"Submit Review"**

### Editing Your Review

- You can edit your review within **24 hours** of posting
- After 24 hours, reviews are locked

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does it take for a technician to accept?**  
A: Usually within 10-30 minutes. If no one accepts within 1 hour, we'll notify you.

**Q: Can I request a specific technician?**  
A: Not currently. The system assigns technicians based on availability and proximity.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Cash on completion, credit/debit card, or app wallet.

**Q: Is there a cancellation fee?**  
A: No fee if cancelled before technician starts traveling. Otherwise, you may pay the visit fee.

**Q: How do I get a receipt?**  
A: After payment, go to the completed order ‚Üí **"Download Receipt"**

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the service?**  
A: Contact support through the app. Go to **"Help"** ‚Üí **"Contact Support"**
